How to Pick

  • Choose 2 golfers from each bracket
  • 8 golfers total per entry
  • Each golfer can only be in one bracket
  • Submit picks before the lock deadline
📊

How Scoring Works

  • Your 8 golfers' tournament scores are combined
  • Lowest combined score wins the sweep
  • Missed cut (MC) penalty applies
  • Withdrawal (WD) penalty applies
⚖️

Tie-Breaker

  • If scores are tied, tie-breaker applies
  • Tie-breaker is entered at time of submission
  • Closest tie-breaker to the actual result wins
  • Contact the pro shop if you have questions
🏆

Prizes

  • Top 3 places win prizes
  • Wooden Spoon for last place
  • Results announced at tournament end
  • Final standings on the sweep page
